About This Project

This project is a virtual try-on demo built with Django and MediaPipe FaceMesh.

It lets you try on fun accessories like glasses, hats, and earrings in real-time. The face landmarks are tracked in your browser so the accessories move naturally with your head.

You can reset the camera anytime or download a snapshot of your look.
